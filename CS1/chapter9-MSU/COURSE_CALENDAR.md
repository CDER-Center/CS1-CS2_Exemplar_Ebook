## Calendar Notes

- Friday, August 29 was the first day of Session A and followed a Monday
  schedule; therefore, the course met that day.
- Monday, September 1 was Labor Day.
- Fall Break was October 18–26.
- Thanksgiving Break was November 26–30.
- Monday, December 15 followed a Thursday schedule, so this Monday/Wednesday
  section did not meet.
- Wednesday, December 17 was used as a makeup class for assignments and
  examinations.

## PDC Activity

The PDC component was implemented on **Wednesday, December 3, 2025**, through
the **Flag Maker** unplugged activity. The activity introduced parallel
computing concepts, including data parallelism, through a collaborative flag
animation task. All materials required for the activity are located in
[`Unplugged_Activities/FlagMaker/`](Unplugged_Activities/FlagMaker/).
